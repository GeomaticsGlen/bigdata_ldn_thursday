🎤 Speaker Spotlights

Dr. Ayesha Malik – DeepMind

AI Governance Lead at DeepMind. Her keynote explores ethical boundaries in autonomous systems and how to design for accountability and transparency.

Adam Sroka – Hypercube

CEO of Hypercube. Speaking on agentic orchestration and the future of composable data platforms.

Head of Engineering. Sharing insights on scaling data infrastructure for real-time analytics and AI deployment.

🚇 Tube Reading: Key Themes

  • Agentic AI: Autonomous systems with minimal human oversight—debates on control and accountability.
  • Observability: Tools like Arize offer visibility into model drift and decision paths.
  • Governance: Platforms like Collibra and Immuta enforce policy across decentralized agents.
  • Data Mesh & Lakehouse: Snowflake and Databricks are redefining multi-cloud data architecture.
