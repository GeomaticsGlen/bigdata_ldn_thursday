🚀 Geospatial Highlights for 2025

  • AI-Driven GIS: Platforms now use machine learning to extract features, classify imagery, and predict spatial trends.
  • Real-Time GIS + IoT: Sensors stream live location data into GIS platforms for traffic, energy, and public safety.
  • Digital Twins & 3D GIS: Virtual models of cities and infrastructure enable simulation and planning.
  • Cloud-Native GIS: Scalable platforms like ArcGIS Online and BigQuery GIS support massive spatial datasets.
  • Environmental Monitoring: GIS is central to climate modeling, deforestation tracking, and disaster response.
  • Geospatial Analytics Market: Valued at $38.3B in 2024, projected to reach $118.1B by 2034 with 13.6% CAGR.

🧠 Questions to Ask Exhibitors

AI & ML Integration
  • “How does your platform support geospatial feature extraction or classification using AI?”
  • “Can you demonstrate ML models trained on satellite or mobility data?”
Cloud & Scalability
  • “What’s your approach to scaling raster/vector workloads in cloud-native pipelines?”
  • “Do you support real-time streaming of location data from IoT sensors?”
Data Types & Interoperability
  • “Which spatial formats and coordinate systems do you support natively?”
  • “Can your system integrate with open-source GIS tools like QGIS or PostGIS?”
Smart Cities & Urban Analytics
  • “How do you support spatial analysis for traffic, infrastructure, or mobility planning?”
  • “Do you offer digital twin capabilities for urban environments?”
Environmental & Sustainability
  • “Can your platform ingest and analyze remote sensing data for climate or land-use monitoring?”
  • “Do you support temporal-spatial modeling for environmental change detection?”
Governance & Privacy
  • “How do you handle privacy concerns around location-based data?”
  • “Is there support for anonymizing or aggregating geospatial data for compliance?”
